About Us

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a national-level professional body dedicated to enhancing the quality of technical education in India. It aims to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical application by encouraging innovation, leadership, and professional growth among students. The ISTE Students‚Äô Chapter, NIT Durgapur, provides a dynamic platform for students to explore learning beyond classrooms through technical workshops, guest lectures, and flagship initiatives such as Career Insights and Training (CIT).
